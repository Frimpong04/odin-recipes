Finished Lasagna Recipe

A well presented lasagna recipe

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 teaspoons salt, divided
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 container ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided

Directions

  1. step 1

    2. preheat the oven to 375 degree F. Grease an 8-inch baking dish wish cooking spray

  2. step 2

    3. Pat dry zucchini slices with a paper towel to get rid of excess moisture

  3. step 3

    4. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat.Add ground beef; cook until browned, 5 to 8 minutes.Add marinara sauce, 1 teaspoon salt, oregano, and pepper; simmer for 10 minutes

  4. step 4

    5. Combine remaining 1 teaspoon salt, ricotta cheese,egg, and nutmeg in a bowl; mix well

  5. step 5

    6. make 1 layer of zucchini slices in the prepared baking dish. Cover with 1//2 of the sauce. Add another layer of zucchini slices. Spread ricotta mixture on top. Sprinkle with 1 cup mozarella cheese.Add another layer of zucchini slices; cover with the remaining sauce and top with 1 cup mozarella cheese and Parmesan cheese. cover baking dish with aluminium foil

  6. step 6

    7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove aluminium foil and bake until top is golden, about 15 minutes more

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 424 calories; protein 30.4g; carbohydrates 14.9g; fat 26.8g; cholesterol 117.4g; sodium 1426.6g.
