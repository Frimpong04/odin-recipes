Roasted Chicken With Skillet Stuffing

Sliced French bread soaks up roasted chicken juices to make delicious skillet stuffing. Perfect for chilly winter evenings. Since it uses one pan, cleanup is a breeze

Quick stats

  • Prep: 40mins
  • Cook: 1 hr 15 mins
  • Additional: 10 mins
  • Total: 2 hrs 5 mins
  • Servings: 6
  • Yield: 1 chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup butter, divided
  • 3 large onions, sliced
  • 1 leek, sliced
  • 2 teaspoons lemon zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • 1 whole chicken, rinsed and patted dry
  • Korsher salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 loaf French bread, cut into 3/4-inch slices
  • 1/4 fresh lemon juice
