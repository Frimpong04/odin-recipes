Chicken Shawarma

A finished chicken shawarma wrap

It is light and filling. it is divine

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons white vineger
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons garlic paste, divided
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • i tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 3 potatoes, cut into fries
  • oil for frying
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 8 pita breads, split
  • 1 cup pickled cucumbers

Directions

  1. Mix vineger, soy sauce, 1 teaspoon garlic paste, ginger paste, salt, and pepper together in a shallow dish. Add chicken breasts; turn to coat.Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon vegetavle oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Remove chicken breasts from the marinade and cook until browmned and no longer pink in the cebter, 6 to 8 minutes per side. Transfer to cutting board; slice into thin strips
  3. Mix remaining 1 teaspoon garlic paste with myonnaise in a bowl
  4. spread garlic mayonnaise inside pita breads. Fill each pita with French fries, chicken slices, and pickles
