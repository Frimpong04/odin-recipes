Eggplant Sandwiches

Roasted eggplant in a French roll with feta cheese, basil and garlic mayonnaise. This is my favorite sandwich of all time

Ingredients

  • 1 small eggplant, halved and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil, or as needed
  • 1 cup of mayonnaise
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 French sandwich rolls
  • 1 small tomato sliced
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven's boiler. brush eggplant slices with olive oil, and place them on a baking sheet or boiling pan. Place the pan about 6 inches from the heat source. Cook under the boiler for 10 minutes, or until tender and toasted
  2. Split the French rolls lengthwise, and toast. In a cup or small bowl, stir together the mayonnaise and the garlic. Spread this mixture on the toasted bread. Fill rolls with eggplant slices, tomato, feta cheese and basil leaves

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 802 calories; protein 23.8g; carbohydrates 93.3g; fat 39.5g; cholesterol 3.8mg; sodium 1460.2mg
