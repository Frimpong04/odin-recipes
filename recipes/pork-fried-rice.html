Pork Fried Rice

this is a quick and easy meal to prepare and it tastes great

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 boneless pork loin chop, cut into small pieces
  • 1 cup chopped broccoli
  • 1 green onion chopped
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 1 cup cold cooked rice
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
